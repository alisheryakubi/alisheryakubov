AI-Powered Marketing

Practical AI workflows that help hospitality and brand teams move faster without sacrificing quality — content ideation, drafting, editing, visuals, and distribution.

The approach

Removing repetitive friction, not replacing creativity

AI-assisted workflows combine large language models, image generation tools, automation platforms, and structured prompts to support content ideation, drafting, editing, and distribution. The goal is not to replace human creativity but to remove the repetitive friction that slows it down.

A well-designed AI workflow can generate ten headline options, produce a first draft of a blog post, resize a hero image, or summarize guest feedback. The strategist then steps in to choose, refine, and align the output with brand voice and business goals.

Current experiments include prompt libraries for hospitality copy, automated content calendars, AI-assisted email campaigns, and visual asset generation for social media. Each experiment is documented — what works, what fails, and why — turning individual tests into reusable team knowledge.

What is included

Capabilities

  • Hospitality copy prompt libraries and reusable templates
  • Automated content calendars and scheduling workflows
  • AI-assisted email campaign drafting and audience segmentation
  • Visual asset generation for social media and ads
  • Guest feedback summarization and sentiment tracking
  • Editorial review and brand-voice alignment
How it works

A simple, repeatable process

1. Define the objective. Awareness, occupancy, revenue, or reputation — every workflow starts with a clear business goal.

2. Build the workflow. Select models, prompts, and automation steps. Test on real samples before scaling.

3. Review and refine. A human strategist chooses, edits, and aligns the AI output with the brand. The workflow improves with each cycle.

4. Document and reuse. What works becomes a repeatable asset for the team. What fails becomes a documented lesson.
