Overview

What This Service Includes

Good design is not only about how something looks — it is about how clearly it communicates. Graphic design services cover digital campaigns, social media, print materials, presentations, promotions and branded content, with each piece planned around a specific business or communication goal.

Types

Types of Graphic Design

  • Social media post and story design
  • Ad creative for digital campaigns
  • Print materials (flyers, brochures, menus)
  • Presentations and pitch decks
  • Branded templates
  • Promotional materials
  • Packaging and label concepts
  • Event and signage graphics
Scope

What Is Included

  • Concept and direction
  • Layout design
  • Typography and color application
  • Brand consistency
  • Print-ready or digital file preparation
  • Source files where agreed
  • Revisions within the agreed scope
How it works

Process

1. Brief. We collect the objective, audience, brand and required deliverables.

2. Concept. One or two design directions are proposed.

3. Design. Approved concepts are produced across the required formats.

4. Review. Feedback is collected and revisions are completed.

5. Delivery. Final files are prepared in the agreed formats.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide source files?
Source files can be included when agreed in the quotation.
Can you match our existing brand?
Yes. Brand guidelines are followed throughout.
Do you handle printing?
Print-ready files are provided; printing can be coordinated separately.
What is the turnaround?
Depends on scope — small projects from a few days, larger sets from one to two weeks.
