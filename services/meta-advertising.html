Overview

What This Service Includes

Posting on social media does not always guarantee that the right people will see your offer. Meta advertising allows businesses to promote services, offers and content to selected audiences on Facebook and Instagram. Campaigns are customized according to the client's industry, target market, available content and business objective.

Who This Service Is For

  • Service-based businesses

  • Event organizers
Scope

What Is Included

  • Campaign strategy and objective definition
  • Audience research and targeting setup

  • Creative direction for visuals

  • Weekly or monthly optimization
  • Performance reporting
  • Budget management and recommendations
How it works

Process

1. Discovery and goal-setting. We define the business objective — awareness, enquiries, messages, visits or sales.

2. Audience and offer planning. Targeting, offer and creative direction are planned around the goal.

3. Tracking and pixel setup. The Meta Pixel, conversions and any CRM integrations are configured.

4. Creative production. Ad copy and visuals are produced by the relevant specialists.

5. Campaign launch. Campaigns are launched with a structured testing plan.

6. Optimization. Audiences, creative and budgets are adjusted based on performance.

7. Reporting. Regular reports show what is working and what is being improved.
