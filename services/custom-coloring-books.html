Turn Your Brand, Story or Idea into a Custom Coloring Book

Overview

What This Service Includes

A custom coloring book can be more than a children's activity. It can become a branded guest experience, educational product, promotional gift, event activity, retail item or memorable way to introduce children and families to a business. Clients can provide a complete concept or begin with a simple idea — we help develop the storyline, page structure, activities and visual direction.

Size

Book Size

Projects can range from approximately 25 to 200 pages, depending on the concept, purpose, budget and delivery timeline. Large books and complex illustrated projects will require a longer production period than smaller books.

Timing

Timeline

A standard smaller project may begin from approximately one week after the concept and materials are approved. Large books, high page counts, complex characters and printed orders will require additional time.

Revisions

Revisions

Standard coloring-book projects include two structured revision rounds. Additional revisions, major storyline changes or changes requested after approval may require an additional fee.

Rights

Commercial Rights

Commercial-use rights are available for businesses and clients who intend to distribute or resell the completed book. The exact rights, exclusivity and permitted use must be confirmed in the project quotation.

How it works

Process

1. Client submits the idea and requirements. Tell us what the book should feature, who it is for, and when it is needed.

2. We discuss the audience, purpose, style and page count. A short call or brief defines the direction before production begins.

3. The team develops the concept and page structure. Storyline, activities and visual direction are planned and approved.

4. The visual direction is approved. Characters, style and layout are confirmed before pages are produced.

5. The coloring book is designed. Pages, cover and activities are created by the relevant specialists.

6. The client reviews the first version. Feedback is collected for the included revision rounds.

7. Included revisions are completed. Adjustments are made within the agreed revision scope.

8. Final digital or print-ready files are delivered. Files are prepared in the agreed formats.

9. Printing is arranged when included in the quotation. Print coordination is available as a separate line item.
