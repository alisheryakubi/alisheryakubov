Overview

What This Service Includes

A website is often the first place a potential customer checks before making contact. A modern, responsive site needs to load quickly, read clearly on mobile, present the right information, and guide visitors toward an enquiry, booking or purchase. Sites are built around the client's goals — not a generic template.

How it works

Process

1. Discovery. We define the site's goal, audience and required pages.

2. Structure and content. Page structure and content are planned before design.

3. Design. Visual design is produced and approved.

4. Development. The site is built responsively and tested across devices.

5. Integration. Forms, analytics, WhatsApp and SEO basics are configured.

6. Review and launch. The client reviews, approves, and the site goes live.

7. Handover. Documentation and basic training are provided.
