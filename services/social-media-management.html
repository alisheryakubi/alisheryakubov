Overview

What This Service Includes

Consistent social media presence requires planning, design, writing, scheduling and reporting — work that most businesses struggle to maintain internally. Monthly social media management covers the full cycle so the client's channels stay active, on-brand and measurable without adding to the internal workload.

Scope

What Is Included

  • Monthly content planning
  • Post and story design
  • Caption writing in English or Russian
  • Hashtag research
  • Scheduling and publishing
  • Community management (basic replies)
  • Monthly performance report
  • Strategy adjustments
How it works

Process

1. Strategy and calendar. We plan the month's content around goals and audience.

2. Production. Design and copy are produced for each post and story.

3. Approval. The client reviews and approves before publishing.

4. Publishing. Content is scheduled and published at the agreed times.

5. Engagement. Basic community management keeps the channel responsive.

6. Reporting. A monthly report shows performance and next steps.
