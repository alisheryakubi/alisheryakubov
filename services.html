The approach

A Flexible Agency Built Around Your Project

Alisher Yakubov operates a flexible creative and digital agency serving businesses, professionals and individuals in the UAE and internationally.

Instead of maintaining a large traditional agency structure, each project is handled by a selected team of specialists based on the work required. This allows the agency to remain fast, efficient and affordable while still providing access to the right skills for design, advertising, websites, automation, content and customer communication.

Clients communicate through one central point of contact while the work is coordinated across the relevant specialists.

The process

How We Work

  1. 1. Tell Us What You Need
    The client submits a project enquiry describing the required service, goals, preferred timeline and available materials.
  2. 2. We Review the Project
    Alisher reviews the requirements and determines the work, team members, timeline and expected deliverables.
  3. 3. You Receive a Quotation
    The client receives a customized quotation based on the actual project requirements. Prices are not fixed publicly because every project may require a different amount of work.
  4. 4. Project Confirmation
    Once the quotation is approved, the required upfront payment is made and the project begins. Most customized projects require a 50% deposit. Selected smaller services, fixed packages, third-party services and urgent projects may require 100% payment in advance.
  1. 5. The Team Begins the Work
    Alisher coordinates the selected specialists and ensures that the work follows the approved direction.
  2. 6. Review and Revisions
    The client reviews the work and submits feedback according to the revision terms included in the quotation.
  3. 7. Final Delivery
    The final files, access, campaign, website or completed service are delivered after final approval and payment.
