Will AI Search benefit the direct online channel or the OTAs?

Alisher Yakubov contributed an expert view to Hospitality Net's Digital Marketing in Hospitality World Panel alongside other hospitality, technology and digital strategy leaders. His contribution explained how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and other AI platforms operate through different commercial and discovery models. Alisher Yakubov Shares His View on the Future of Search Marketing in Hospitality

Is Traditional Search Marketing Dead?

Alisher Yakubov joined Hospitality Net's Digital Marketing in Hospitality World Panel to discuss whether traditional search is becoming irrelevant. His published view argued that search is evolving rather than disappearing. He outlined a three-layer strategy for hospitality brands: traditional search for conversion, AI search for visibility and social discovery for capturing demand earlier in the customer journey.
