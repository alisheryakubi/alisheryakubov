About

Who is Alisher Yakubov?

Alisher Yakubov is a hospitality marketing professional, AI creator and digital strategist based in the United Arab Emirates. He serves as Cluster Assistant Marketing & Communications Manager across Fujairah Rotana Resort & Spa and Nour Arjaan by Rotana, supporting marketing strategy, communications, digital content and brand visibility for both properties.

Alongside his hospitality work, Alisher explores practical applications of artificial intelligence in marketing and content creation, with a focus on improving creative workflows while preserving authentic brand communication.

He is also a digital creator and AI creator, producing content about marketing, technology, hospitality, and the practical uses of AI across YouTube, LinkedIn, and his own website.
