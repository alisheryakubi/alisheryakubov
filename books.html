About the reader

An interactive flipbook experience

Each book on this page opens in a digital flipbook reader that mirrors the feel of a real illustrated book. The cover shows first; click to open it, then swipe or use the arrows to turn through the pages.

The reader works on desktop, tablet, and phone. On larger screens you see a two-page spread; on phones you read one page at a time. Pages turn with a soft paper-swipe motion, and you can zoom, open thumbnails, or enter fullscreen for a closer look.
