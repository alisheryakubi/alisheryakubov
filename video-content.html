Video Content

Educational explainers, hospitality case studies, behind-the-scenes production notes, and short-form social video — built to test what resonates with different audiences.

The approach

A learning lab and a distribution channel

Video content serves two purposes: it is a learning laboratory for production, and it is a distribution channel for ideas that help others. Content ranges from educational explainers about AI tools for marketers to hospitality case studies, behind-the-scenes production notes, and personal reflections on building a digital career.

The formats are intentionally varied to test what resonates with different audiences. Video production also sharpens skills in pacing, thumbnails, scripting, and audience retention — skills that translate directly into better hotel video content, social reels, and advertising creative.

What is included

Capabilities

  • Educational explainer videos about marketing and AI tools
  • Hospitality case studies and property storytelling
  • Behind-the-scenes production notes and process videos
  • Short-form social video and reels
  • Scripting, thumbnail design, and audience-retention strategy
  • Channel planning and content calendar
How it works

From idea to published video

1. Define the story. What should the audience learn, feel, or do after watching?

2. Script and plan. Write a tight script, plan the shots, and design the thumbnail before recording.

3. Produce. Record, edit, and grade. Keep pacing brisk and the first frame intentional.

4. Publish and learn. Track retention, test formats, and refine the next video from the data.
