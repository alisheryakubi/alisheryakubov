Custom Books

Independently published children's books with interactive flipbook previews — content development, production, publishing, and marketing, managed end to end.

The approach

From idea to a book readers can open in the browser

Independently published children's books explore digital publishing as a creative channel. Each title is brought together with an interactive flipbook preview — visitors can open the cover and swipe through sample pages directly in the browser.

The flipbook reader is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It preserves the feel of a real illustrated book with soft paper-swipe page turns, while working smoothly on desktop, tablet, and phone.

Publishing workflows cover content development, illustration, production, listing optimisation, and distribution — managed end to end so each title has a clear path from manuscript to reader.

What is included

Capabilities

  • Content development and manuscript editing
  • Illustration direction and page layout
  • Production and file preparation for print and digital
  • Listing optimisation — SEO, keywords, and categories
  • Distribution and marketplace setup
  • Interactive flipbook preview for the web
